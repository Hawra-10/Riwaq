Riwaq

A meeting place of emotions and arts that touch the heart

Vision

To be the leading Arab platform that empowers new insights to discover and develop their artistic talent, and to enrich the cultural scene by making art and creative education available to all in an inspiring and stimulating environment.

Mission

Our mission is to build a vibrant Arab art community by providing high-quality inclusive art workshops covering a wide range of disciplines for all ages and levels, creating a safe and inclusive learning environment where every individual feels supported and encouraged to express their creativity without restrictions.
